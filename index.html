Hey there! I'm Diana.

I'm currently a junior industrial designer pursuing a human-computer-interaction minor at Carnegie Mellon. An avid believer of learning by doing, I'm constantly looking for opportunities to apply user centered design in both the physical and digital world. I think design is a tool for me to create delightful and easy experiences for people, and to solve even the smallest of problems. I'm inspired by the works of Sebastian Errazuriz, Norman Bel Geddes, Massimo Vignelli and BRTHR.

Outside of design, I enjoy trying new recipes, petting dogs, mastering the art of guac making, and finding new adventures. I have coded this website and hope to improve upon it as I learn more html and css. I love to explore new things whenever I can and try to fill every day with laughter. I believe the best experiences are those shared with others, and I want my designs to impact those special social interactions.
