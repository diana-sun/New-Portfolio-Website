Luminaire

A light that encourages balanced meal-time conversations.

Overview

Conflict in families often arise when there is a lack of understanding between family members, and more specifically, parents and teens. While it is important that we do not prevent these potentially valuable conflicts, we want to raise conversational awareness at the dinner table. With Luminaire, the value of the lamp is determined by how each user responds to the changing of the light and adjusts their speech. In essense, Luminiaire is a focus on social sustainability, and aims to help facilitate conversatons between family members by encouraging equal conversation so that they may learn more about each other.
