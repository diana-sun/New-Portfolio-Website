Form & Fire

A context-specific fire extinguisher with intuitive interaction.

Process

After researching the geometries associated with a modern dental office, I designed a fire extinguisher that was inspired by dental equipement, and fit within the environment. First I used the subtle curves and edges of dentist tools to create a form, and then changed subtleties of it to better fit into a clean and sleek dental office. I abstracted a tooth related inspiration to create an intuitive interaction as well. The fire extinguisher not only required the product to be context specific, but also ergonomic and easy to use.

Interaction

I designed an interaction where the safety feature mimicked the closing of the jaw. When the safety was pressed, the weight of the top of the extinguisher would close the "jaw", and enable activation. The activation button and gauge were placed in easily visable areas. The wall mount prevents the safety feature from being accidentally unlocked and adds an easy to find red color to the extinguisher.
